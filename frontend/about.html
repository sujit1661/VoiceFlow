About Flow

Built by one person,
for everyone

Flow is a voice-to-text AI application that turns natural speech into polished writing — in any app, on any device. It was built from scratch by Sujit Sadalage using a FastAPI backend, Groq's Whisper API, and LLaMA 3.1 for AI polishing.

SS
Sujit Sadalage
Builder & Creator of Flow

Hi, I'm Sujit

I built Flow because I was frustrated with every voice dictation tool I tried — they either produced messy output full of filler words, or they only worked inside one specific app.

I wanted something that worked everywhere — Gmail, Slack, VS Code, Notion — with a single hotkey. And I wanted the output to be genuinely clean and well-formatted, not just raw speech-to-text.

So I built it. Flow uses Groq's ultra-fast Whisper API for transcription and LLaMA 3.1 for AI polishing. The whole thing runs locally on your machine — your voice data never leaves your control.

Design Principles

What I believe

Every product decision in Flow reflects these principles.

01
Speed above everything
The moment between speaking and seeing clean text should feel like magic. Sub-second latency isn't a nice-to-have — it's the whole product.
02
Works everywhere or nowhere
A voice tool that only works in one app is a gimmick. Flow types into any text field on your entire system. That's the only acceptable standard.
03
Privacy is non-negotiable
Your voice stays on your machine. Your API key is yours. No accounts, no subscriptions, no data hoarding. Flow is a tool, not a surveillance product.
04
Free and open
Flow is open source and free to run. The best tools should be accessible to everyone, not locked behind a $30/month paywall.
05
Distraction-free UX
Hold, speak, release. That's the entire interaction model. No onboarding wizards, no mode switches, no mental overhead.
06
Built for real work
Flow is designed for developers, writers, and professionals — people who type thousands of words per day and need a better way.
